## What We Know About Preet

- Founder & CEO, StafGo Health
- MBA Finance/Marketing, Brunel University London
- 18+ years in financial leadership
- Co-founded StafGo with Dr. Jasmine Gill (physician)
- Publicly stated goal: "Sovereign AI Agent for the legacy USA dental market"
- StafGo offers: insurance verification, dental/medical billing, credentialing, PPO negotiations, denial management, prior auth, patient billing, RCM reporting
- Hybrid on-shore/off-shore model (US + India)
- Explicitly positions as AI-driven RPA + BI analytics company
- Believes 100% AI automation without human oversight fails — wants AI + expert ops team
